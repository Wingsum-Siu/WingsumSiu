About Me!

All about me!


Middle school Wing Sum Siu was so obsessed with anime that she woke up at 5 in the morning to catch up on airing seasons. That's how crazy she was. She messed up her perfectly planned sleep schedule and created a bunch of other problems for her future self. Although she now rarely watches anime these days, she will stay caught up with most sequels of shows she enjoyed in the past. She's definitely grown to enjoy other things more, such as playing rhythm games and playing games on the Switch. However, she's noticed a pattern. The games have anime characters in them. Hmm...maybe nothing has changed.

Why Does This Site Exist?

Purpose Audience Justification
This site exists so you can compare your watch time to the average! If you're unsure if your watch habits are too extreme, feel free to use this site to help clarify(not really). Anime watchers! But people who watch other genres can give this a try too! Sometimes, it becomes really easy to spend lots of time in front of the screen because there are just too many shows to watch! Sometimes, one could become so engrossed that they neglect their responsibilities. The site can also be used as a wake up call as well.
